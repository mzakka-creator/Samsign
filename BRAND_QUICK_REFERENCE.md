# SAM SIGN - Quick Reference Guide

## Brand Essentials

**Name:** SAM (SamSign)  
**Tagline:** "Art & Experience.. Driven by Performance."  
**Established:** 2018  
**Industry:** Professional Advertising Solutions & Signage

---

## Colors

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Yellow/Gold** | `#F4C430` | Primary brand color, CTAs, highlights |
| **Black** | `#000000` | Primary text, logo, headers |
| **White** | `#FFFFFF` | Backgrounds, negative space |
| **Light Grey** | `#F5F5F5` | Backgrounds |
| **Medium Grey** | `#808080` | Secondary text, borders |
| **Dark Grey** | `#333333` | Subtle accents |

---

## Typography

- **Headings:** Bold, geometric sans-serif (large, confident)
- **Body:** Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Style:** Generous letter-spacing, clear hierarchy

---

## Brand Personality

- Professional yet creative
- Performance-driven
- Art meets engineering
- Industrial strength with artistic vision
- Established credibility

---

## Design Principles

1. **Bold but not loud**
2. **Simple but not basic**
3. **Professional but not corporate**
4. **Modern but not trendy**

---

## Website Sections

1. **Hero** - Full viewport, bold headline, CTA, signature work
2. **Work Philosophy** - 2-3 core principles with icons
3. **Partners** - Logo grid, grayscale with color on hover
4. **Vision & Values** - Forward-thinking vision, 3-4 value pillars
5. **Footer** - Contact info, links, social media

---
